Hani's is a bakery and cafe that takes classic nostalgic American treats and gives them a elevated taste. I tried the cinammon roll, which was great. The bun was soft and the cinnamon was not overpowering, but a little too sweet. I also tried the smoked salmon flatbread. The salmon was fresh, but the flatbread was dry and felt stale. I want to go back to try the famous chocolate chip cookie!

  • Vibes: It's a cozy atmosphere, but can get busy and seats are limited.
  • What to get: Malted Cinammon Bun
  • Price: $10-20
